Radhanagari Dam

Radhanagari Dam, is a gravity dam on Bhogawati river near Radhanagari in the state of Maharashtra in India. Construction was initiated by visionary Rajarshi Shahu on 18 February 1907.The dam is being used for irrigation as well as hydro-electricity. The dam is located amidst scenic surrounding in the backdrop of thick forest cover.

Rautwadi Waterfall

Rautwadi is around 100 feet tall waterfall that leaps into a pond at its feet. A well-paved way lands you at the feet of the waterfall. The paved way is around half a km to 1 km from where you park your vehicles. During the peak of the monsoon season, the water flow is quite massive. It creates a fog of minuscule water droplets which is an amazing site. Right from the entrance, you can hear the gushing sound of the waterfall, which goes on rising as you go near the waterfall.


Mahalaxmi Mandir

Ambabai Temple (also known as Mahalaxmi Mandir) is a Hindu temple dedicated to Lakshmi, who is believed to reduce there as Supreme Mother Mahalakshmi and is worshipped by locals as Ambabai. Mahalakshmi is the consort of Vishnu and it is customary among Hindus to visit Tirumala Venkateswara Temple, Kolhapur Mahalakshmi Temple and Padmavathi Temple as a yatra (pilgrimage).
